Our Story

Where African heritage meets modern vision

Sheymineegud was born from a deep reverence for Africa's rich textile traditions and a burning desire to redefine luxury fashion for the modern world. Every thread we weave carries the spirit of our ancestors and the ambition of a new generation.

From the vibrant markets of Lagos to international runways, our collections celebrate identity, craftsmanship, and the timeless elegance of African-inspired design.
